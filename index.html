Prayogam 2025

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower."

Venue: University Auditorium | Timings: 10 AM - 5 PM

Overview

The Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering, Poornima University (PU), Jaipur organized the Annual Technical Project Expo “Prayogam-2024” on the occasion of National Science Day. The exhibition was intended to motivate students to apply the theoretical concepts in the form of projects. More than 1200 students participated in the exhibition and over 220 projects were displayed. Projects were focused on real world problems in the areas of AI, Data Science, Game Technology, IOT, Cyber Security, Web Technologies and Mobile applications.
